Build your own cheat card (steal this)

Made with one structure anyone can copy. The whole idea: don't memorize answers, prep anchors you can talk from.

1
Pick your 3 power stories that fit this exact job. Pin them on top, lead with them, bend most answers back to one. One real number each.
2
Find their one line. Read the company's own words (press release, site, JD), pull one phrase that's also true of you, and mirror it back.
3
Per question, write 3 things, not a script: a one-to-two-sentence SAY line, three HIT keyword anchors, and the number to land.
4
STAR, but short: Situation (1 line) → Task → Action (the bulk) → Result (the number). Most people forget the Result, it's the whole point.
5
Say it out loud once. A mock, a voice memo, a friend. Reading ≠ saying. Tighten anything over 60 seconds.
